Game rules:

  • Paint all grid with the same color
  • Each cell you click and his neightbors will invert they color
  • Be fun!

Some tips:

  • Basic movements:

    By clicking on a cell, it will change its color and that of its neighbors.
    This means that you can use it at the ends of the board to make a corner or side change the color. If a cell only has 3 neighbors because it is in a corner, you can make a 2x2 square disappear (1)
    If, on the other hand, you click on a side, you will affect a group of 2x3 (2)
    Finally, if you click in the center of the board, it will affect the maximum possible cells, inverting a 3x3 square (3).
